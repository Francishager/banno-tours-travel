Package Overview

Witness one of nature's greatest spectacles - the Great Migration in Tanzania's Serengeti National Park. This 6-day safari adventure combines the endless plains of the Serengeti with the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Ngorongoro Crater.

Experience millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles in their annual journey across the vast Serengeti ecosystem. Our expert guides will position you perfectly to witness river crossings, predator-prey interactions, and the raw beauty of African wildlife in their natural habitat. The tour includes luxury tented camps, all game drives, and optional hot air balloon safaris.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport and transfer to Arusha. Meet your safari guide and get briefed about the upcoming Serengeti adventure. Overnight at a luxury lodge with views of Mount Meru.

Meals: Dinner | Accommodation: Luxury Lodge Arusha
Day 2: Arusha to Central Serengeti

Early departure via Ngorongoro Conservation Area to Central Serengeti. First game drive en route with opportunities to spot resident wildlife. Afternoon arrival at luxury tented camp in the heart of Serengeti.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Serengeti Tented Camp
Day 3: Full Day Serengeti Game Drives

Migration spectacular day! Full day game drives following the Great Migration herds. Witness dramatic river crossings (seasonal), predator hunts, and endless plains teeming with wildlife. Optional hot air balloon safari at sunrise.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Serengeti Tented Camp
Day 4: Serengeti to Ngorongoro

Morning game drive in Serengeti before departing to Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Afternoon visit to a authentic Maasai village to learn about traditional culture and lifestyle. Evening at crater rim lodge.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Ngorongoro Crater Lodge
Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

Africa's Garden of Eden! Full day exploring Ngorongoro Crater - the world's largest intact caldera. High concentration of wildlife including Big Five, flamingos at soda lake, and unique crater ecosystem.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Ngorongoro Crater Lodge
Day 6: Return to Arusha & Departure

Final breakfast with crater views before departure to Arusha. Optional visit to local markets for souvenirs and cultural crafts. Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport for departure or onward connections.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Accommodation: None (Departure day)
