Package Overview

Discover Kenya's crown jewel - the Masai Mara National Reserve, world-famous for its exceptional population of lions, leopards, cheetahs and the annual migration of zebra, Thomson's gazelle, and wildebeest from the Serengeti.

This 5-day safari adventure offers guaranteed Big Five sightings in one of Africa's most spectacular wildlife destinations. Experience the dramatic Mara River crossings (seasonal), witness predator-prey interactions, and immerse yourself in authentic Maasai culture. Our luxury tented camps provide comfort in the heart of the wilderness while expert guides ensure unforgettable wildlife encounters.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi

Arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and meet your safari guide. Transfer to luxury hotel in Nairobi for safari briefing and overnight accommodation. Optional visit to Giraffe Centre or David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage.

Meals: Dinner | Accommodation: Luxury Hotel Nairobi
Day 2: Nairobi to Masai Mara

Early departure to Masai Mara via the Great Rift Valley with photo stops at escarpment viewpoints. First game drive en route to camp with opportunities to spot resident wildlife. Evening arrival at luxury tented camp.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Mara Tented Camp
Day 3: Full Day Masai Mara Game Drives

Big Five safari day! Full day game drives exploring different areas of the reserve. Search for lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, and rhinos. Witness the Great Migration river crossings (seasonal) and predator-prey interactions.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Mara Tented Camp
Day 4: Mara Game Drive & Maasai Cultural Visit

Culture meets wildlife! Morning game drive followed by visit to an authentic Maasai village to learn about traditional lifestyle, customs, and the coexistence of people and wildlife. Evening game drive for more wildlife viewing.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Mara Tented Camp
Day 5: Return to Nairobi & Departure

Final early morning game drive to catch wildlife at their most active. Return to Nairobi with lunch en route. Optional shopping for souvenirs and local crafts. Transfer to airport for departure or optional Nairobi extension.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Accommodation: None (Departure day)

Frequently Asked Questions

Masai Mara is excellent year-round. July-October offers the Great Migration river crossings, while November-June provides excellent game viewing with fewer crowds and green landscapes during calving season.

While wildlife is wild and unpredictable, Masai Mara has one of the highest concentrations of Big Five in Africa. Our expert guides and multiple game drives significantly increase your chances of seeing all Big Five animals.

Pack neutral-colored clothing, comfortable walking shoes, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, camera with extra batteries, binoculars, and light jacket for early morning game drives. We provide a detailed packing list upon booking.
