Package Overview

Experience the ultimate wildlife encounter with endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. This 5-day adventure takes you deep into Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, one of Uganda's most pristine wilderness areas and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Our gorilla trekking safari offers an intimate wildlife experience like no other. You'll spend precious time with gentle giants in their natural environment, guided by expert trackers and rangers who know the forest intimately. The journey includes luxury eco-lodge accommodation, all gorilla permits, professional guides, and cultural interactions with local communities.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kampala

Arrival at Entebbe International Airport and transfer to Kampala. Meet your safari guide and get briefed about the upcoming adventure. Overnight at a luxury hotel in Kampala.

Meals: Dinner | Accommodation: Luxury Hotel Kampala
Day 2: Transfer to Bwindi

Early morning departure to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. Scenic drive through Uganda's countryside with stops for photography and local interactions. Evening arrival and check-in at eco-lodge.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Bwindi Eco Lodge
Day 3: Gorilla Trekking Day

The highlight of your safari! Early morning briefing followed by gorilla trekking in Bwindi Forest. Spend one magical hour with a gorilla family in their natural habitat. Evening relaxation and cultural entertainment.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Bwindi Eco Lodge
Day 4: Community Visit & Nature Walk

Visit local Batwa communities to learn about their traditional forest lifestyle. Guided nature walk to discover unique flora and fauna. Bird watching opportunities in one of Africa's most biodiverse forests.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Accommodation: Bwindi Eco Lodge
Day 5: Return to Kampala

Final breakfast at the lodge and departure to Kampala. Stop at the Uganda Equator for photos and souvenir shopping. Transfer to Entebbe International Airport for departure or optional Kampala city tour.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Accommodation: None (Departure day)
